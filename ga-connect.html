Attribution

5 Attribution Models Built In

Choose the model that fits your business. Switch anytime to compare how credit is distributed across your customer journey.

First Touch

100% credit to the first interaction. Great for understanding what fills the top of your funnel.

Last Touch

100% credit to the final touchpoint before conversion. Shows what closes deals.

Linear

Equal credit distributed across every touchpoint. Fair representation of the full journey.

Time Decay

Recent touchpoints get more credit (7-day half-life). Ideal for short sales cycles.

Position-Based (U-Shaped)

Popular

40% first touch, 40% last touch, 20% split across middle. Balances discovery and conversion.
