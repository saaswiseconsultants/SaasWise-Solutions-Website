How It Works

From Address to Full Property Record

Fetch on demand or schedule monthly auto-refreshes.

1

Address

Address is read from any source record (Contact, Lead, Opportunity, or custom) or entered manually in the property card.

2

Fetch

One click calls your enabled Attom API endpoints. Data is mapped to 195+ Salesforce fields and child records.

3

Enrich

Record is upserted to Attom_Data__c and automatically linked to the source record. Child records (sales history, schools, permits) are created in bulk.

4

Display

Property card shows valuation, equity, and details right on the record page. Schedule batch syncs to keep data fresh automatically.
